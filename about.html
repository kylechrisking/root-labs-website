Our Story

Root Labs was founded by James Samford and Kyle King, two IT professionals with a shared vision of providing innovative and tailored IT solutions to businesses of all sizes. With over 10 years of combined experience in the industry, we've seen firsthand the transformative power of technology when applied strategically.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to bridge the gap between complex technology and practical business needs. Today, we're proud to offer a comprehensive range of services that help our clients navigate the ever-evolving digital landscape.
