About Root Labs

Our Mission

Root Labs is a technology innovation hub founded by James Samford and Kyle King, dedicated to developing cutting-edge solutions that drive business success and positively impact your business, and customers. By harnessing expertise in Web Development, Cybersecurity, Software Development, Emerging Technologies, Additive Manufacturing, and Engineering & Robotics, we create accessible and sustainable technologies that empower individuals and organizations to achieve their goals. Rooted in the belief that technology should be a force for good, we strive to leverage our skills and resources to address environmental and social challenges, creating a more equitable and sustainable future.
